Casula at a glance:

  • Suburban planning is well-thought with easy access to all facilities and main roads.
  • Shops: Crossroads shops, Costco, Casula Mall and Casula shopping centres cover the everyday needs of residents. 5-min from Liverpool Westfield. Kmart is open 24H. Few restaurants and bars are open to the public.
  • Schools: families enjoy Casula public and private schools, primary and high catholic schools and plenty of child care centres. One of the Top secondary schools - Hurlstone Agricultural High School Glenfield is just next door. Safe and quiet area with low traffic and low crime level. Great to raise kids.
  • Transport: cars, bus stops are spread around the suburb and Casula, Glenfield or Liverpool train stations. Casula is only 34 km or 40 min drive from Sydney CBD
  • Health care: general practitioners and dentists are easy to get appointments and the area is very close to Liverpool Hospital.
  • Casula Powerhouse Arts Center attracts visitors from all over South-Western Sydney and has become a must-visit cultural destination in the area with its art exhibitions and events, but also fantastic Bellbird restaurant and bar.
  • Casula Parklands is the ultimate playground for kids and adults, and our furry friends: you can walk, you can play, you can ride flying foxes and just have fun. On a nice day, you can have a picnic here with family and friends.
  • To enjoy 1 to 2hr walking trails, morning runs or cycling, head to Leacock Regional Park and follow Bellbird walking track. Amazing andscapes and views accompanied by cute chirping birds. The council is planning to link Liverpool to Parramatta with a new cycling trail by 2023, and create more cycling paths, including Horningsea Park Route (From Austral to Liverpool), Prestons Route, West Hoxton Route and many more.
